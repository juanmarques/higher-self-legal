Higher Self — Privacy Policy

Effective date: 16 May 2026 · Last updated: 16 May 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the Higher Self mobile application (the “App”) collects, uses, and protects your personal information. By using the App you agree to the practices described here.

1. Who we are

“Higher Self” is operated by Juan Marques (the “Developer”, “we”, “us”). You can reach us at jmarques@ennov.com for any privacy questions or to exercise your rights described below.

3. How we use your information

  • To operate the App and provide the features you sign up for.
  • To synchronise your content across your devices.
  • To process and validate in-app purchases and subscriptions.
  • To send the notifications you have opted in to.
  • To diagnose crashes, fix bugs, and improve performance.
  • To enforce our Terms of Service and prevent abuse.
  • To comply with our legal obligations.

We do not sell your personal information, and we do not use your content to train third-party AI models. AI-generated responses in the App are produced by an inference service (see Section 4) which processes your prompt to return an answer; that service does not retain your prompts for training.

4. Third-party processors

We rely on the following service providers to operate the App. Each acts as a data processor under our instructions:

  • Supabase Inc. — backend hosting, authentication, and storage of your account and content. See the Supabase Privacy Policy.
  • RevenueCat, Inc. — subscription management and entitlement validation. See the RevenueCat Privacy Policy.
  • Google LLC — Google Play Billing for in-app purchases; Google sign-in if you choose it; Firebase Cloud Messaging for push notifications. See the Google Privacy Policy.
  • Apple Inc. — App Store Billing and Sign in with Apple if you choose those flows. See the Apple Privacy Policy.
  • MiniMax — AI inference service that powers conversational replies. Prompts you send to the persona chat feature are transmitted to MiniMax for completion. See the MiniMax Privacy Policy.

5. Legal bases (EEA / UK users)

Where the EU or UK GDPR applies, our legal bases are:

  • Contract — to provide the App and services you signed up for.
  • Legitimate interests — to operate, secure, and improve the App.
  • Consent — for push notifications and optional features you explicitly enable.
  • Legal obligation — to comply with applicable laws.

6. Data retention

We retain your account and content for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account from inside the App, your content is removed within 30 days. Backups and aggregated, non-identifying analytics may persist for an additional period to satisfy legal and operational requirements. Purchase records required for tax reporting are retained for the period required by law.

7. Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to:

  • access the personal data we hold about you;
  • correct inaccurate or incomplete data;
  • delete your data (subject to legal-retention exceptions);
  • export your data in a portable format;
  • object to or restrict certain processing;
  • withdraw any consent you previously gave; and
  • complain to your local data-protection authority.

To exercise any of these rights, email jmarques@ennov.com. We respond within 30 days.

8. Children

The App is intended for users aged 13 and over. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, contact us and we will delete it. The App is not directed at children and does not participate in Google Play’s Designed for Families programme.

9. Security

We use industry-standard safeguards including TLS encryption in transit, row-level security on our database, and access controls on operational systems. No system is perfectly secure; if a breach affects your data we will notify you and the appropriate regulators as required by law.

10. International transfers

Our processors operate in the United States and the European Union. When personal data is transferred outside your country we rely on adequacy decisions or Standard Contractual Clauses to safeguard the transfer.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be announced in the App or by email. Continued use of the App after the effective date of the updated policy means you accept the changes.

12. Contact

Privacy questions, deletion requests, or any other concern: jmarques@ennov.com.
